Cup Noodles

The saviour of broke college students all over the world

A cheap, quick and easy meal

Ingredients

  • A package of instant cup noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil water in kettle or over stove, whichever is more convenient
  2. Peel back lid of the noodles halfway
  3. Add boiling water until water reaches the line inside the container
  4. Close lid and keep covered for however long is noted on the packaging
  5. Remove lid completely, add soup powder (if included), stir and enjoy!
